CARHART

Carhartt is an American brand established in 1889, renowned for its high-quality, durable, and functional workwear. Known for garments such as jackets, overalls, and pants, Carhartt expertly combines rugged durability with a sense of style, making its clothing a staple not only in demanding work environments but also in urban and street fashion scenes.

KITH

Kith is a New York-based luxury streetwear brand founded by designer Ronnie Fieg, widely recognized for its minimalist urban style and exclusive collaborations. Known for blending premium fashion with strong streetwear influences, Kith has positioned itself as a cultural icon in both the sneaker world and modern lifestyle. Each collection brings together high-quality materials and innovative design.

NIKE

Nike is a global brand in sports apparel and footwear, celebrated for its groundbreaking innovation in performance technology and style. With its iconic logo and the powerful slogan "Just Do It," Nike has become a universal symbol of motivation, pushing boundaries in both athletic excellence and urban fashion. Nike continually redefines standards through advanced design and high-quality materials.

OFF-WHITE

Off-White is a luxury urban fashion brand founded by Virgil Abloh, renowned for its cutting-edge style and signature use of industrial graphics and distinctive quotation marks. Known for blending elements of streetwear with high fashion, Off-White has become a key reference in contemporary culture and a major influence on youth fashion.

PALACE

Palace is a British streetwear brand deeply inspired by skate culture, known for its bold, irreverent style and its iconic triangle logo. Established with a focus on capturing the essence of skateboarding, Palace integrates classic skateboarding influences with a modern urban aesthetic, creating unique pieces that resonate widely. The brand's designs reflect a blend of playful attitude and innovative design.

SCRAPWORLD

Scrapworld is a Mexican streetwear brand that fuses urban style with both local and global cultural influences, creating a distinctive identity in the fashion world. Known for its bold designs and unique graphics, Scrapworld has carved out a place in youth fashion, celebrated for its authenticity and creative spirit. Each collection reflects a dynamic blend of cultural elements, often drawing from Mexican heritage.

STÜSSY

Stüssy is a pioneering streetwear brand founded in California, recognized for its unique style rooted in surf, skate, and urban culture. With its signature logo that resembles a hand-drawn signature, Stüssy has made a lasting impact on urban fashion since the 1980s. The brand combines elements from California’s beach and skate scenes with influences from hip-hop, punk, and global subcultures.

SUPREME

Supreme is a New York-based streetwear brand founded in 1994, widely recognized for its bold aesthetic and exclusive collaborations with prominent artists, designers, and brands. Known for its iconic red box logo with white lettering, Supreme has become a powerful status symbol within urban and skate culture. From its early days catering primarily to skateboarders and artists.

Gran Vía, 48, Centro, 28004 Madrid

The location at number 48 on Gran Vía is ideal for a streetwear pop-up store due to its high foot traffic and reputation as a shopping and fashion center in Madrid. This area combines modernity with historic charm, making it a magnet for urban brands aiming to connect with trend-focused consumers.

Moreover, its proximity to other fashion stores, entertainment spots, and cafes creates a vibrant atmosphere that attracts a young, diverse crowd, perfectly aligned with the streetwear aesthetic. The steady flow of people and the visibility of the street provide an excellent opportunity for brand exposure and product promotion in an energetic, contemporary urban setting.
